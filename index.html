A Little About Me

I am a master's student in Digital and Interaction Design at Politecnico di Milano, bringing a background in interactive design to the world of UX/UI. My passion is translating complex ideas into elegant, user-friendly interfaces.

I love experimenting with new technologies like SVG animation and front-end frameworks to create unique and engaging digital experiences, always aiming for accessibility and visual harmony.
