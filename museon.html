Muse*on: Streamlining Small Museum Management

Muse*on is a digital platform designed to simplify operations for small museums, providing centralized tools for efficient management.

Project Summary

Objective: Develop an all-in-one software to streamline museum operations and enhance resource management.

Role: UX/UI Designer: research, wireframing, prototyping, testing, final delivery.

Tools: Figma.

Problem Statement

Small museums lack efficient tools, leading to resource and workflow inefficiencies. Muse*on aims to address these challenges with a centralized platform.

Research Insights

Research Activities

  • Contextual Inquiry: Observed staff workflows.
  • Interviews: Conducted 12 interviews.
  • Surveys: Collected quantitative data.
  • Ecosystem Mapping: Visualized department interactions.

Ideation & Prototyping

Wireframes & Prototypes

We started with sketches, then refined them into wireframes and high-fidelity prototypes, iterating based on feedback to ensure user needs were met.

User Testing

Usability Testing

Two rounds of usability testing with 8 participants led to improvements in navigation and task prioritization features.

  • Navigation: Simplified for easier use.
  • Task Management: Color-coded priorities for better clarity.
